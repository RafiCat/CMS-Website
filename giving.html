Strengthen Lehigh, Change Your World

You know what's special about Lehigh: its exceptional learning and living environment, a tradition of hands-on application of knowledge, and the way students, faculty, and researchers easily cross disciplinary boundaries as they pursue innovative solutions to today's grand challenges. As you think about how Lehigh changed you, consider how you can change the lives of others through giving to Lehigh.

  • Have immediate impact on students and programs through annual giving to the Lehigh Fund.
  • Uphold talented scholars through financial aid and scholarship and rich university experiences.
  • Encourage innovative breakthroughs by faculty and researchers.
  • Strengthen Lehigh's reputation and the value of a degree.

Contact us at (800) 523-0565 for more information on how you can make an impact.
